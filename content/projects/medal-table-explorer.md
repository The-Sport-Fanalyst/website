## Overview

The final medal table hides the story of how it was won. This app lets you scrub through a Games and watch the standings shift event by event.

## Features

Hour-by-hour playback, country and sport filters, and a "what if" mode that recomputes the table under alternative medal-weighting schemes. Built to be embeddable on any sport page.
