## Overview

3x3 is fast, high-variance, and perfect for simulation. This project runs tens of thousands of tournament rollouts to produce medal probabilities and seeding sensitivity.

## How to help

We need help calibrating the game-level scoring model to the 21-point / 10-minute format, and adding the wheelchair basketball bracket. Issues are tagged good-first-issue.
