Gymnastics is judged, not timed — which makes it one of the most analytically interesting sports at the Games. Scores separate into difficulty (D) and execution (E), inviting study of strategy, consistency, and judging patterns.

## Why it's a great starting point

The D-score / E-score split gives a built-in structure for modeling risk-reward, while open scoring archives support research into scoring trends and consistency.

## What lives here

Scoring datasets, research on difficulty inflation and judging consistency, all-around prediction models, and routine-composition explorers.
