Swimming pairs a tight, repeatable race format with high event volume — heats, semis, and finals across dozens of events generate a steady stream of split-level data every meet.

## Why it's a great starting point

Lap splits, reaction times, and turn data are increasingly public. The structured progression from heats to finals is perfect for win-probability modeling and fatigue analysis.

## What lives here

Open split datasets, research on taper and turn efficiency, finals-prediction models, and lane-by-lane race visualizers — for both Olympic and Paralympic programs.
