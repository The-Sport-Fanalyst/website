Olympic basketball brings the deepest tactical analytics toolkit of any Games sport — possessions, lineups, shot quality — now applied to national-team play and the fast-growing 3x3 format.

## Why it's a great starting point

Play-by-play and box-score data are abundant. Tournament structure and small samples make for interesting bracket-modeling and upset-probability work.

## What lives here

Tournament datasets, research on pace and lineup efficiency, bracket and medal models, and shot-chart and possession dashboards — including wheelchair basketball.
