Athletics is the backbone of the Summer Olympics and Paralympics, and the richest playground for sports analytics. Every event produces clean, comparable numbers — times to the hundredth, distances to the centimeter — across a century of records.

## Why it's a great starting point

Results are standardized, deeply historical, and globally tracked. That makes athletics ideal for prediction models, progression curves, and fairness studies across Para classification systems.

## What lives here

The athletics hub collects open datasets (historical finals, season bests, wind readings), research on performance progression and pacing, predictive models for LA28 finals, and interactive tools like split-time calculators and medal projections.
